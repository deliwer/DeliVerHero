1. Core Value Stack
Your “Why this exists” + “Why it’s irresistible” in one package.

Tagline:

“Trade your old iPhone for AED 1,000+ in premium water — become a Dubai Planet Hero.”

Key Pillars:

Instant Tangible Reward: Premium AquaCafe water starter kit on the spot.

2. User Journey Funnel
Step 1 — Awareness:

PR headlines + influencer posts: “Dubai startup swaps your phone for water.”

Street-level co-marketing: Posters at gyms, cafés, and eco-markets with QR to Hero signup.

Step 2 — Curiosity Hook:

QR or link lands on Hero Challenge landing page (headless Shopify).

Countdown timer + leaderboard + “Only 100 Founding Hero spots.”

Step 3 — AI Concierge:

User chats with embedded AI agent:

Asks what phone they have.

Instantly calculates trade-in reward.

Books pick-up/drop-off time.

Generates personalized “Hero Profile.”

Step 4 — Conversion Trigger:

Limited Hero spots visible (social proof + scarcity).

“Double reward if you join before midnight” banners.

Step 5 — Fulfillment & Share Loop:

Trade-in completed → user receives Hero Kit → AI generates:

Personalized impact story (“You saved 312 bottles of plastic”).

Shareable badge + video clip for Instagram/TikTok.

Referrals bump them up leaderboard.

3. Tech + Shopify Headless Build
Base Stack:

Shopify (products, checkout, fulfillment).

Headless storefront (Next.js / Hydrogen for speed + control).

Embedded AI Concierge (OpenAI API / Shopify Functions for instant calculations).

Airtable / Firebase for leaderboard + impact tracking.

Key Pages:

Hero Challenge Landing Page — countdown, leaderboard, join button.

AI Concierge Widget — conversational trade-in process.

Impact Dashboard — real-time liters saved + CO₂ avoided.

Founding Hero Wall — profile pics & ranks.

4. Gamification Layer
Game Mechanics:

Leaderboard — points from:

Trade-in (base points).

Referrals (bonus points).
